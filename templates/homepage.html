How it works?

Scrapmart works in four simple steps

1-Create an Account:

It is simple. From the above buttons just click on the Sign Up button, Enter the required details and Boom! Your Scrapmart account is created!

2-Login:

After getting your account created, just click on the login button from the homepage or from navigation bar.

3-SEARCH:

Use the Search from the Userhomepage Dashboard and search for Scrap dealers by choosing your City and locality. View the profile to get shop details.

4-DONE:

Once you get the contact details, Contact the Scrap Dealer using the obtained information and get rid of the scrap!
